About Paveways

Paveways is a government-supported initiative designed to make urban travel in Kerala seamless, affordable, and accessible for everyone. Our app integrates real-time public and private transport data into a single, easy-to-use platform.

Our Mission

To empower commuters with accurate travel information, helping them save time, reduce costs, and travel safely.

Our Vision

To become Kerala's most trusted travel companion, fostering sustainable mobility and smart city living.

Core Benefits

  • Real-time updates on buses, trains, autos, and cabs.
  • Official and transparent fare details.
  • Tourist-friendly route planning with safety features.
