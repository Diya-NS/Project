Frequently Asked Questions

What is Paveways?

Paveways is a travel companion app that helps you plan, compare, and track multi-mode journeys across Kerala in real time.

Does Paveways work offline?

Some features like route saving work offline, but live tracking and fare updates require an internet connection.

Is the fare data accurate?

Yes, all fare data is sourced from official Kerala transport bodies and updated regularly.

How can tourists benefit from Paveways?

Tourists can use Paveways to find guided routes, safe travel options, and detailed fare breakdowns for various transport modes.
